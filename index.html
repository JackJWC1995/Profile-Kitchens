Who We Are

A trusted name
in Devon kitchens

Based in Devon and operating across the whole of the South West, Profile Kitchens has been transforming homes for over three decades. Behind the business is one highly experienced kitchen fitter — a craftsman whose attention to detail and breadth of skill means every project, large or small, is handled with genuine care.

We are fully qualified and registered to certify both gas and electrical works, meaning you receive a truly complete service. There's no need to coordinate multiple tradesmen or worry about who does what — we handle it all, keeping disruption to an absolute minimum.

From the very first measurement through to tiling, flooring, plastering, and every last finishing detail, Profile Kitchens is with you every step of the way. No corners cut. No fuss.
